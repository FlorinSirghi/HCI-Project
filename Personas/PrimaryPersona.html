Detailed Description

Remus is a software engineer working for Microsoft on their well-known operating system, Windows. He developed a passion for computers ever since he was a kid when he was playing video games.

He enjoyed shooter games the most, because they were pretty straight-forward : kill the enemy to win the game and they didn't have too many rules, as he was spending his time learning for school and he didn't want to spend anymore time on reading a lot of rules or familiarizing himself with complex mechanics, but the lack of realism in these games frustrated him : when he was shooting his gun he thought that the recoil is too big, the movement didn't feel natural and there were a lot of cheaters.

In his free time now, Remus enjoys spending time in nature by himself or with his friends, playing board games or having a meal.

During a workshop at the company about physical health he realized that he sits a lot in front of his computer to work and also this might be the reason for his back pain and that he needs some physical activity in his life to compesate for this. The workshop also encouraged the participants to do some introspection and try to align the activities they might do with their personality traits or other things they would like in their life. Thus, when Remus thought of activities he might do he realized that he has a competitive spirit and that he needs to improve his communication skills to be able to work in a team better.
