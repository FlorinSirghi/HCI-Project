Detailed Description

Paul is a fitness trainer at Oxygen, during his work he helps people understand how to do the exercises correctly and how to use the various machines in the gym. He also prepares meal plans and fitness plans for his clients. His favourite thing to do in the gym is running on the treadmill training for speed and endurance.

At the gym, Paul is well known for his focus on doing the exercises with correct form. He never cuts corners or ego lifts just to prove that he is "strong". He always gives constructive criticism to his clients and never tries to lie to them just to make them fell better.

Before being a fitness trainer, Paul was a darts referee, he enjoyed both playing and watching, especially observing infractions and ensuring adherence to the rules. Due to his trustworthy nature, he was very succesfull and was often called to referee darts matches not only in his city but also all around the country. However the lack of physical activity required for this job made him quit as it didn't quite align with his passions.

Lately, the owner of Oxygen gym started to replace the old equipment with newer things. The new equipment is based on newer technology discovered in fitness and so it requires a higher technology level to be able to use it correctly. This comes as a challenge to Paul as he was never a "tech" guy. Nevertheless, this will not stop him to fulfill his passion for fitness.
